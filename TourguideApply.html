How to Apply?

Welcome! We are currently seeking individuals with the potential to become a tour guide for our ATV tour company. Do you have any prior experience as a tour guide? Alternatively, if you lack experience but aspire to be a member of our company, we have two application options available. You can either submit your resume online or apply in person at our company location (Sapinit Road Barangay San Juan, Antipolo, Rizal).Kindly review the requirements and skills necessary for this role. We also provide training for new employees who are just starting out as tour guides. Please take a moment to read the details below for more information.

    Requirements:

  • 1. Proficient Communication Skills: Tour guides need to effectively communicate with tourists, provide clear instructions, and share interesting information about the ATV tours.
  • 2. In-Depth Knowledge: A strong understanding of the local area, attractions, trails, and landmarks is essential for providing informative and engaging tours.
  • 3. Physical Fitness: Tour guiding often involves physical activities like leading hikes or outdoor adventures. A certain level of physical fitness is important.
  • 4. Safety Consciousness: Safety is a top priority. Guides should be well-trained in safety protocols, first aid, and emergency procedures.
  • 5. Customer Service Skills: Tour guides should be friendly, approachable, and capable of creating a positive and enjoyable experience for tourists.
  • 6. Adaptability: Tours may encounter unexpected situations. Guides should be able to adapt and make decisions on the spot.
  • 7. Language Proficiency: Depending on the region, fluency in multiple languages can be a significant advantage for serving a diverse range of tourists.
  • 8. Cultural Awareness: Respect for different cultures, customs, and sensitivities is crucial when interacting with a diverse group of tourists.
  • 9. Problem-Solving: Guides should be resourceful and capable of resolving unexpected issues or challenges that may arise during a tour.

    Skills:

  • 1. Navigation Skills: Tour guides need to know the trails, routes, and geography of the area to lead tourists safely.
  • 2. Storytelling: The ability to tell engaging stories and convey historical or cultural information in an interesting manner enhances the tour experience.
  • 3. Time Management: Guides must keep tours on schedule to ensure that all planned activities are covered.
  • 4. Interpersonal Skills: Building rapport with tourists, answering questions, and fostering a positive atmosphere is essential.
  • 5. Public Speaking: Confidence in speaking to groups, projecting voice, and maintaining enthusiasm is crucial for leading engaging tours.
  • 6. Environmental Awareness: Promoting responsible tourism and educating tourists about the importance of protecting the environment is valuable.
  • 7. Mechanical Skills: Basic knowledge of ATV mechanics and troubleshooting can be helpful in addressing minor technical issues during tours.
  • 8. Basic First Aid: Being trained in basic first aid techniques allows guides to provide initial assistance in case of injuries.
  • 9. Orientation Skills: Guides should be able to guide tourists and keep track of their location, especially in outdoor settings.
  • 10. Sales and Marketing: While not essential, having the ability to upsell additional services or provide information about the ATV rental business can be advantageous.

Things to Know!

    A. Things to know when you are applying:

  • -Visit our ATV Rental website where you can upload your resume.
  • -Upload your resume and provide a contact email for communication.
  • -Submit your resume.

    B. Things to know if you have already submitted your resume:

  • -Please await a response from our admin. You will receive a call and email regarding the interview schedule.
  • -As part of the interview process, be prepared to present how you handle situations when a customer violates the company's rules and regulations.
  • -You can visit our social media accounts for more information and ideas.

    C. Things to know if you are in the interview stage:

  • -The interview will be conducted via Zoom. You will receive an email containing the Zoom link to join the interview.
  • -Ensure a stable internet connection and have your camera turned on during the interview.
  • -Have confidence in yourself and demonstrate the requirements and skills provided on our website.

    D. Things to know if you have been accepted as a Tourguide in the Company:

  • -Await a schedule to visit the company for tour guide training.
  • -Study the provided document files from the employer.
  • -Prepare for training by learning about the announcements the admin will send through email and SMS.

    E. Things to know if you are undergoing training:

  • -Pay close attention to the instructions.
  • -Take note of important details that need to be remembered.
  • -Always keep a positive attitude and remember to pray.
